About

Velumie is an independent visual studio focused on landscape photography and digital asset development. The work explores light, atmosphere, and natural environments through a refined artistic perspective.

Photography

The studio specializes in landscape photography including oceans, mountains, night skies, and remote environments.

Selected works are distributed globally via online platforms as digital downloads and fine art prints.

Digital Assets

In addition to photography, Velumie develops and manages digital assets, including internet domain names.

These assets may be listed on international marketplaces and transferred to buyers for branding or online business use.

Revenue Model

— Photography sales and licensing
— Domain name transfers via online marketplaces

Payments are processed through established international platforms and financial service providers supporting cross-border transactions.
